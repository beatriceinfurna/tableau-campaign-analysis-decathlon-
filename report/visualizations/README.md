# Visualizations

This folder contains selected **static visual outputs** extracted from the Tableau dashboards developed for the *Decathlon x Sissy Mua influencer campaign analysis*.

The visualizations are included to document the dashboard logic and support the key insights presented in the final report.

---

## Dashboard Overview

### 1. Campaign Profitability Dashboard
This dashboard focuses on evaluating the **financial performance** of the influencer marketing campaign.

The visualizations support the analysis of:
- Campaign revenue generated by the limited-edition products
- Estimated profitability using an assumed average margin
- Comparison between campaign costs and generated value
- Overall assessment of campaign return

This dashboard addresses the business question:
**“Was the influencer campaign profitable for Decathlon?”**

---

### 2. Customer Rejuvenation Dashboard
This dashboard evaluates whether the campaign contributed to **rejuvenating the customer base**, with a focus on younger consumers.

The visualizations highlight:
- Customer age distribution before and after the campaign
- Share of Generation Z customers among campaign buyers
- Demographic shifts linked to the campaign period

This dashboard addresses the business question:
**“Did the campaign succeed in attracting younger customers?”**

---

## Notes

- The visualizations are static exports from Tableau dashboards.
- Interactive features (filters, tooltips, drill-downs) are not available in this format.
- The full Tableau workbook is not publicly shared, as this was a **group academic project** developed in a private course environment.

These visual outputs are intended to complement the final presentation and provide a clear overview of the analytical results.
